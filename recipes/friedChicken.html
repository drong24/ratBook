Fried Chicken

Discription

Korean fried chicken half original half yangnyeom style

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh
  • Flour
  • Gochujang
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken to pieces slightly bigger than your thumb
  2. Season half in salt and pepper and let it sit for 1/2 hour
  3. Mix flour and water (and starch for increased crisp) together until a smooth batter is formed
  4. Dip chicken in batter and fry until golden brown
  5. Mix a gochujang, soy sauce, and sugar in a bowl
  6. Place unsalted chicken and sauce mix in heated pan and mix until chicken is coated in sauce
  7. (Optional) Sprinkle sesame seeds on chicken
  8. Enjoy!
