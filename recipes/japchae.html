Japchae

Discription

Japchae is a korean noodle dish made with sweet potato glass noodles

Ingredients

  • Japchae dry noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Boil japchae noodles till soft
  2. Drain water and add soy sauce and sugar
  3. Add sesame oil and mix
  4. Enjoy!
