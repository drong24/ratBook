Samgyeopsal

Discription

Korean bbq pork belly

Ingredients

  • Pork belly
  • Gochujang
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix Gochujang, soy sauce, and sugar together to make a marinade
  2. Place pork belly into marinade and sit for over half an hour
  3. Place pork belly onto a heated pan, turn over till both sides are evenly cooked
  4. Cut cooked pork belly and serve with rice and kimchi
  5. Enjoy!
